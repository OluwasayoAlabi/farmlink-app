FarmLink - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is FarmLink?
>

FarmLink is an e-commerce platform that connects farmers directly with buyers to reduce post-harvest waste and increase profitability.

Who can use FarmLink?
>

Anyone aged 18 and above who can read and write and has internet access can register as a farmer or buyer.

How do I register?
>

Simply download the FarmLink app or visit the website, click on "Sign Up," and follow the steps to create your account.

Is my data safe?
>

Yes. We use encrypted technology to protect your personal and transaction data.

What if I have a dispute with another User?
>

You can raise a ticket using the support feature. We aim to resolve all issues amicably and promptly.

How do I list a product as a Farmer?
>

After registration, go to your dashboard and click "Add New Listing." Fill in the required details and submit.

Can I delete my account?
>

Yes. You may request account deletion by contacting our support team.
