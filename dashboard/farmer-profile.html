About Farmer

Musa Adewale is a 45-year-old commercial farmer based in Oyo State, Nigeria. With over 35 hectares of farmland, Musa is one of the region's leading producers of cassava, maize, tomatoes, and watermelon. Over the years, he has transitioned from traditional open-market selling to embracing digital tools that connect him directly to urban buyers and wholesalers.
